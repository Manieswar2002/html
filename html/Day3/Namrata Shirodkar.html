Namrata Shirodkar

Namrata Shirodkar is South megastar Mahesh Babu’s wife and former Femina Miss India title in 1993. She has also worked as an Indian actress. The stunner tied the knot with Mahesh Babu in 2005 and has a son 
and a daughter. The actress is known for her aesthetic and stylish clothing sense, but her beautiful white wedding sari has always remained the talk of the town. Let’s take a detailed look at her wedding attire. 
Namrata wore an elegant white silk sari on her wedding day featuring gold and green zari border, and she paired the drape with a half-sleeved blouse in a bottle-green shade. She neatly carried the front plaits,
which looked stunning and perfectly wrapped around her waistline and curves, accentuating her figure and overall look. The golden border had minimal floral and leaf detailing which looked stunning and co-ordinated.
Her jewellery was not over the top, as she kept a simple and elegant match with her outfit. She simply wore a maang tikka set with rubies and diamonds, a beautiful golden and green necklace with gold matching jhumkas 
for earrings. Her bangles matched her gold and emerald choker-style necklace. She wore a simple ring with mehendi-laden hands which looked subtle and traditional. Talking about her hair and makeup details, Namrata opted 
to highlight her big eyes with kohl and wore a grey-bluish shade of eyeshadow with a minimal stroke of eyeliner, coated with heavy mascara. She also added nude lipstick, keeping her cheeks minimally contoured but dressy 
with a beautiful stroke of minimal blush. Talking about her hair, she simply carried a sleek plait adorned with beautiful gajra flowers on the sides.
